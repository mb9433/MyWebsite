Extinct Animals

Dodo

Dodo birds are flightless birds which means that they cant fly.
Dodos were really big birds, they were around 3 feet tall
and could reach a body weight of 20 kg or even more!
Their diet mostly consisted of fruits, nuts and seeds.
It has also been suggested that they might have also been
eating shellfish and crabs.

Ground Sloth

Ground sloths are like really big sloths and they have been extinct
for about 11 thousand years! Ground sloths could also sand up just on
their two legs in order to reach the high branches. Ground sloths could
also run at about 1.68 meters per second and 3.7 miles per hour, this is
around the average walking speed for humans which explains why they might
be easy prey. Ground sloths were actually herbivores and they ate cacti,
yucca, desert globemallow and joshua tree fruits, along with other plants.

Wooly Mammoth

Wooly mammoths look really scary and they look like they would eat humans,
but they are actually herbivores and they eat plants. They have been extinct
for about 4000 years. They lived in tundra steppe, here it went from Northern Asia
to many parts of Europe and North America. Woolly Mammoths were about 3-4 meters
which is definitely not as huge as most people think it is. They weighed at an
average of 7,300 kg which is really heavy concidering they are only 3-4 meters tall.
